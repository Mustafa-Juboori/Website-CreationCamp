Game Development

Overview &
Goal:

  • Game design; Students will learn how to design and develop game mechanics; The rules that govern how a player will interact with the game.
  • Level design; Students will learn the level design techniques to create engaging games based on strong storylines
  • Programming; Students will learn the coding languages and frameworks to build logic into video games
  • Art and animation; Students will learn the tools and skills to design the aesthetics of a game and the characters within the game

  • Real world experience in the tech and video game development industry
  • A competitive skill set for future co-op and career opportunities
  • Experience and certification on industry software and tools
