CreationCamp



App Development | Game Development | Content Creation

Overview and Goal:

  • CreationCamp gives students the opportunity to work alongside industry professionals to learn cutting edge digital skills that are in high industry demand.
  • Students choose an industry that is most interesting to them; video game development, digital content creation or app development.
  • A perfect co-op placement for students to learn in-high demand skills that will give them a leg up for future co-op and career placements.

Students will gain:

  • Real world experience in the technology and startup industry
  • A competitive skill set for future co-op and career opportunities
  • Experience and certification on industry software and tools
